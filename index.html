Neon Midway

Welcome to a technicolor gallery! Targets parade by while you stay stationary. Click or tap to shoot everything worth points and avoid letting them leave.

  • Move your aim with your mouse or finger.
  • Click/tap to shoot. Rapid fire is allowed.
  • Maintain your streak for bonus score.
  • Hit bonus targets for slow-mo and big points.

Controls

Mouse / Touch: aim. Click / Tap: fire.

Keep your streak alive to power up. Hitting glowing prisms slows time for a moment.
